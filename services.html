What Esper Does for YOU!

  • Living Arrangements!

Esper is a networking tool that allows intrepid travellers, seekers of adventure, and even someone just looking for a place to get away from it all to find oppurtunities to travel to places they could only imagine going to otherwise. Locales offer affordable living arangements and all you have to do is set a date!

Of course we make sure to quality check every arrangement, and background check every host, to assure that their eticate and standard of hospitality is up to the Esper standard!

Travel is a big deal, so we make our users aware of potential local travel deals, and of the public transport system. Nothing worse than bording a train in London and mistakenly ending up in Paris! We let you know exactly where your going, and how to get there!

Esper-Plus members, and Gold members get even more! If you subscribe to Esper-Plus, you will be able to sync your account with Lyft, enabling you to get affordable local travel without the hastle of public transport. Gold members can even get to use our tools to nail down affordable flight tickets!

One of Esper's prime goals is to bring the world together via shared expiriences. One way we achive this is by alerting users to travel oppurtunities that coincide with cultural events, ranging from the Fringe Festival in Edinburgh, to Burning man in the american south west!

We also take care to provide sponsored events for our users. Every member has access to culture events sponsored by the Esper Group to expand your horizons! Esper Plus Members get access to exlusive events however, as a thank you for believing in and investing in our cause! Gold members, as an additional thank you, get special access, think backstage access and season pass quality stuff and you'll get the picture!

Latly there is the food, lets say you need some eats but you don't know where to go, or you and your mates cant agree on what to get. We've got you covered, using Esper, you can order, whatever, from wherever (within a reasonable distance) and have it delivered to you!

We take great care to use this tool to bring our users to local shops and restaurants! It is important to support local owners and we make sure to partner with local dining expiriences to bring only the best to our Users! Esper-Plus and Gold subscribers will get access to even higher quality options, with deals offered with partners at the highest class dining your locale has to offer!
